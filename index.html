About ColorEconomy

Tessellated patches represent a dynamic ecosystem with finite food resources. Bold, vivid hues reveal a flourishing world—but as resources dwindle, these colors fade to grey, symbolizing decay and fragility. Odd‑sided herbivores actively seek nutrient‑rich patches, while even‑sided predators pursue prey and gain a speed boost. Creature speed and size vary with vertex count: fewer vertices yield faster, smaller forms; more vertices result in slower, larger ones. When energy is low, both creatures and patches desaturate to grey. Your interactions (clicks) trigger mutations and mass replacement, exposing the delicate balance between diversity and decay.
